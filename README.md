# Hi, I’m Mia


I'm currently a Digital Curator at the British Library. Along the way I've gained degrees and professional international experience as a web and database developer, digital consultant and UX researcher. I love working on open source technologies and open data, especially in museums, libraries, and archives.

---
### Currently working on

- [**Universal Viewer**](https://github.com/UniversalViewer/)  
  Interested stakeholder (2019-now); British Library Product Owner (2015–2019). So happy to see this project continue to thrive as an open source initiative for viewing digital cultural heritage content.


### Past Projects

- [**Living with Machines**](https://github.com/alan-turing-institute/Living-with-Machines/)  
  Digital humanities collaboration exploring the social and cultural impact of industrialization.

- [**Living with Machines: Code**](https://github.com/alan-turing-institute/Living-with-Machines-code)  
  Supporting the research pipeline with reproducible, open research code.
